TECH DICTIONARY

CPU: The Central Processing Unit. It's the "brain" of your computer.
RAM: Random Access Memory. Temporary storage that makes your PC fast.
GPU: Graphics Processing Unit. Handles gaming and video rendering.
HTML: HyperText Markup Language. The code Jett uses to build site skeletons.
SSD: Solid State Drive. Much faster than old hard drives.
